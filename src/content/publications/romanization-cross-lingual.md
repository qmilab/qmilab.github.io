abstract: "We investigate whether script normalization via romanization enables cheaper cross-lingual knowledge transfer from English LLMs. Five typologically diverse languages (Japanese, Hindi, Mandarin, Korean, Vietnamese) are compared across three training conditions (native script, romanized, mixed) using QLoRA fine-tuning on Llama 3.1 8B."
---

This project explores whether romanization reveals a shared probabilistic learning space across languages, or whether script normalization destroys information that matters for downstream task performance.
